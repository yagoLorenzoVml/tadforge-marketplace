# Audience Design

Define:

- Business purpose and downstream destination.
- Eligibility in plain language and exact profile or event logic.
- Market, consent, suppression, employee or test-account, and eligibility exclusions.
- Event sequence, lookback, timezone, qualification, refresh, expiry, and exit behavior.
- Required merge policy and evaluation method.
- Expected size, overlap, freshness, and activation SLA.
- Test profiles or aggregate validation cases without unnecessary personal-data exposure.

Validate field paths, data arrival, Profile enablement, merge behavior, audience dependencies, and destination compatibility. Compare pre-activation and post-activation counts and explain legitimate differences. Do not optimize solely for audience size; optimize for correct eligibility and governance.
