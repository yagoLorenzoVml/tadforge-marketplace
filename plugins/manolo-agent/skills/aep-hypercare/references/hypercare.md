# AEP Hypercare

Define a monitoring window appropriate to the use case and document:

- Change, release time, sandbox, resources, owner, and business consumers.
- Baselines and approved thresholds for ingestion volume, failures, freshness, identity behavior, profile growth, audience size, evaluation duration, activation counts, and destination delivery.
- Check cadence, evidence source, responder, and escalation path.
- Reconciliation from source through destination.
- Known risks, rollback trigger, and recovery steps.
- Exit criteria and handover to business-as-usual operations.

At each checkpoint report green, amber, red, or unknown with evidence. Unknown telemetry is a monitoring gap, not green. Separate platform health from business KPI performance.
