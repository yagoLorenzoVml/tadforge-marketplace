# Governance and Consent Review

Trace governance end to end:

1. Purpose, market, legal basis, owner, and approved consumers.
2. Collected fields and whether each is necessary.
3. XDM and dataset labels, inheritance, and sensitive categories.
4. Enabled policies and whether activation is blocked, warned, or unchecked.
5. Consent source, schema path, timestamp, version, granularity, and update latency.
6. Audience exclusions and destination enforcement.
7. Retention, deletion, access requests, residency, and downstream propagation.
8. Audit evidence and unresolved control gaps.

Absence of a visible policy violation is not proof of compliance. Distinguish technical controls, documented process, and legal approval. Never expose personal data in examples when aggregate or synthetic evidence is sufficient.
